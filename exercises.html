Warm-ups and Cool-downs for Various Sports

Athletics (Track & Field and other running events):

  • Warm-up:

    • Dynamic stretching
    • Jogging
    • Leg swings

  • Cool-down:

    • Slow jogging
    • Static stretching focusing on legs and core

Cricket:

  • Warm-up:

    • Light jogging
    • Arm circles
    • Lunges

  • Cool-down:

    • Gentle jogging
    • Stretching for arms and legs

Motor sports:

  • Warm-up:

    • Neck exercises
    • Arm rotations
    • Seated leg lifts

  • Cool-down:

    • Slow walking
    • Seated stretches for neck and arms

Football (Soccer):

  • Warm-up:

    • Running drills
    • Dynamic stretching
    • Passing and dribbling exercises

  • Cool-down:

    • Light jogging
    • Static stretching focusing on legs and core

Rugby:

  • Warm-up:

    • Running drills
    • Dynamic stretching
    • Passing and tackling drills

  • Cool-down:

    • Slow jogging
    • Static stretching focusing on legs and upper body

Volleyball:

  • Warm-up:

    • Arm circles
    • Leg swings
    • Jumping jacks

  • Cool-down:

    • Slow jogging
    • Stretching for arms and legs

Basketball:

    • Layup drills

    • Dynamic stretching

  • Cool-down:

    • Light jogging
    • Static stretching focusing on legs and upper body

Swimming and Diving:

  • Warm-up:

    • Poolside stretching

  • Cool-down:

    • Slow swimming
    • Stretching in the water

Handball:

  • Warm-up:

Netball:
